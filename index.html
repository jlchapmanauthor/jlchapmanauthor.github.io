About the Author

J.L. Chapman writes horror that does not blink.

J.L. Chapman writes dark psychological horror and supernatural terror built around guilt, regret, fear, and the things people hide behind their best smiles.

His stories dig into haunted places, broken people, moral rot, and the awful moment when a character realizes the monster outside the room might not be the worst thing in the room.
